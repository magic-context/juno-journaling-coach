# Juno the Journaling Coach — Workspace

Welcome to Juno's workspace. This is where your journaling context lives.

## Folder Guide

| Folder | What's Inside |
|--------|--------------|
| `ai-instructions/` | Juno's coaching personality and approach |
| `memory/` | Your journaling profile, patterns, and preferences |
| `knowledge/` | Journaling methods, reflection frameworks, emotional intelligence, wellness |
| `active-projects/` | Current practice, themes, and milestones |
| `historical/` | Past reflections and completed review cycles |
| `templates/` | Journal prompts, weekly reflections, monthly reviews |
| `feedback/` | Notes for improving Juno over time |

## Getting Started

1. Start a conversation with Juno
2. Share what brought you to journaling (Juno will guide gently)
3. Begin your first reflection
4. Journal regularly — Juno notices patterns you can't see alone
